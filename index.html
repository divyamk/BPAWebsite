Attractions

WASHINGTON D.C. WEATHER

The White House

The White House is the official residence of the President. To schedule a pubic tour, you must request it in advance through your Congress Member. At the tour, if you are 18 years or older, you must have a valid photo ID. For more information, click here.
Hours of Operation: Tuesday through Saturday
Prohibited Items: Video cameras, Tablets, Weapons, Food, Liquids, Tobacco
Address: 1600 Pennsylvania Ave NW, Washington, DC 20500


The National Air and Space Museum

The National Air and Space Museum is the world's largest collection of aviation and space artifacts. Admission is free, and there are digital tours available for purchase. Also, there are virtual reality simulators to experience flying airplanes. For more information, click here.
Hours of Operation: 10:00 am to 5:30 pm Every Day
Prohibited Items: Weapons, Food, Drinks(Except bottled water)
Address: 600 Independence Ave SW, Washington, DC 20560



The Lincoln Memorial

The Lincoln Memorial is the most visited site in the National Mall, located on the western side. There is no fee for visiting the National Mall. There are guided tours available free of charge. For more information, click here.
Hours of Operation: Always Open
Address: 2 Lincoln Memorial Cir NW, Washington, DC 20037


The United States Capitol

The United States Capitol is one of the most recognizable symbols of representative democracy in the world. The guided tours do not need to be reserved in advance. The Capitol Visitor Center has many more activities for guests to enjoy. For more information, click here.
Hours of Operation: 8:30 am to 4:30 pm Monday through Saturday
Prohibited Items: Weapons, Food, Drinks, Aerosol containers
Address: First St SE, Washington, DC 20004


The National Zoological Park

The National Zoological Park is one of the oldest parks in the United States. The zoo aims to "provide engaging experiences with animals and create and share knowledge to save wildlife and habitats." The zoo has many different species, including giant pandas. For more information, click here.
Hours of Operation: 8:00 am to 5:00 pm Every Day
Address: 3001 Connecticut Ave NW, Washington, DC 20008
